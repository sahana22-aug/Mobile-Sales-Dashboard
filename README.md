# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across different cities, brands, payment methods, and customer ratings.

The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales trends, transaction volume, product performance, and customer satisfaction, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall mobile sales performance.
- Analyze sales across different cities.
- Track monthly sales and quantity trends.
- Compare mobile brand performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze payment method preferences.
- Evaluate customer ratings and satisfaction.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales by City
- Interactive map visualization.
- Displays total sales distribution across various cities in India.
- Helps identify high-performing sales locations.

### 2. Monthly Quantity Trend
- Line chart showing total quantity sold by day and month.
- Helps track sales fluctuations over time.

### 3. Sales by Mobile Model
- Bar chart comparing sales across different mobile models.
- Identifies top-selling products.

### 4. Transactions by Payment Method
- Pie chart visualization.
- Payment methods analyzed:
  - UPI
  - Debit Card
  - Credit Card
  - Cash

### 5. Customer Rating Analysis
- Displays customer satisfaction ratings.
- Helps evaluate product and service quality.

### 6. Sales by Day
- Trend analysis based on weekdays.
- Identifies peak sales days.

### 7. Brand Performance Table
- Displays:
  - Brand Name
  - Total Sales
  - Total Quantity
  - Total Transactions

### 8. Model-wise Price Analysis
- Gauge chart displaying average product pricing.

### 9. City-wise Sales Comparison
- Waterfall chart comparing city contributions to total sales.
